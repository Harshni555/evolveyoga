Essence of yin yoga

Essence of Yin invites you to slow down, unwind, and journey inward. In this soothing, meditative practice, each pose is held for an extended period, allowing your body to gently open and release. It’s the perfect balance to a fast-paced life, offering deep relaxation, flexibility, and calm. Breathe deeply, connect with your inner self, and experience the true essence of yin.

Mind body harmony

Find your balance and reconnect with yourself in this calming, restorative yoga class. Through gentle movements and mindful breathing, Mind & Body Harmony helps you release tension, quiet the mind, and realign with inner peace. Perfect for all levels, this class invites you to nurture both body and spirit, creating a sanctuary of calm within.
